Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

Rich, decadent, and impossibly fudgy—these brownies are everything a chocolate lover dreams of.

Ingredients

  • • 200g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
  • • 175g unsalted butter, cubed
  • • 3 large eggs
  • • 275g granulated sugar
  • • 85g all-purpose flour
  • • 40g cocoa powder
  • • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • • ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. 1

    Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a 9x9 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang for easy removal.

  2. 2

    Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water, stirring occasionally until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

  3. 3

    In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, sugar, and vanilla until pale and slightly thickened. Pour in the melted chocolate mixture and gently fold until combined.

  4. 4

    Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Fold gently with a spatula until just combined—don't overmix!

  5. 5

    Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the top is set but the center still has a slight wobble.

  6. 6

    Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before lifting out and cutting into squares. This ensures they set properly and achieve that perfect fudgy texture.

Chef's Tip

The secret to ultra-fudgy brownies is slightly underbaking them. They should still have a gentle wobble in the center when you remove them from the oven. As they cool, they'll firm up to that perfect gooey texture. If you prefer a more cake-like brownie, bake for an additional 5-7 minutes.
